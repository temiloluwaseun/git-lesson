Different types of Operators are:-

  • Arithmetic Operators i.e +, -, /, *, %
  • Comparison Operators i.e greater than, less than, greater than or equal to, less than or equal to, !=, !==, ==, ===,
  • Assignment Operator i.e =
  • Logical Operators i.e &&(and), ||(or), !not
  • Type Operators i.e typeof, instanceof
